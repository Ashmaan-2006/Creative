Orientation Week Events

The Highland Games

The Highland Games are an epic display of strength, skill, and community spirit. It’s a time when first-year students come together to compete in fun and challenging activities, from tug-of-war to log tosses, all while celebrating the Queen's Engineering pride!

Thundermugz

Thundermugz is our signature event where we come together to compete in a fun, high-energy chugging challenge. It’s all about teamwork, tradition, and a little bit of friendly competition. Watch the energy rise as we cheer each other on!

The Grease Pole

The Grease Pole is one of Queen's most iconic traditions. It’s a test of courage, determination, and teamwork. The goal is simple: climb the slippery pole and capture the flag, all while the whole school cheers you on from below.
